Doji Jewelry & Gems
Rebrand

Brand Identity | Autumn 2020

A rebrand for Doji Jewelry & Jems including a logo redesign and minor edits on their existing identity. This is an hypothesis project and is not a commissioned project by Hnh Luu.

Doji is about being inspired and carrying your idea through such development, design, and production. It's about making the real estate of wearing jewelry. Whatever your inspiration is, find a way to make your dream come to life.

A matter of creating a bridge between creation of art and creation in art craft design.

Hypothetical Project

The solution

We created a emerald- center colored identity system with a logo that was inspired by emerald, and gems. It showcases the luxury vibe, elegence attitude, and the combination of tradition and modern values that Doji Jewelry & Gems represents.

Logotype

Pagination is a process that is used to divide a large data into smaller discrete pages, and this process is also known as paging. Pagination is commonly used by web applications and can be seen on Google. When we search for something on Google, it shows the results on the separated page; this is the main idea of the pagination.

Typography

Antic Didon- the typeface chosen for this visual identity. The letterform retains curved edges and smooth feeling, but at the same time, full in its body and morphe in between. Cormorant Garamont- the secondary typeface, the traditionally serif font is the perfect fit for Doji brand.
