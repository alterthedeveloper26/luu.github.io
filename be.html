BE LOGO DESIGN

Logo Design | Autumn 2020

Present the core value through the brand logo

We created a bold, purple and orange colored identity system with a logo that was inspired by speed, time, and the road. It has been showcased the dynamic movement progressive attitude, and the agile velocity that Be brand represents.

Logo sketch

I have created 6 different type-base logos then narrow down to 3 options. Considering these three logos and figure out which one strucks me about the agile velocity.
I finally took the logo above, because it gives me the most feel of motion. The sense of the E-branches makes it feel like a ray of light and a distinct brand awareness. Furthermore, when an object moves in slow motion leaving a blurry effect. I want my logo to feel both practical and brand-worthy, the logo will also work well with all platforms of digital and print.
